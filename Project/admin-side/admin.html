💰 How much does teeth whitening cost?
Professional teeth whitening starts from €120 per session. Results last 12–18 months. We also offer take-home kits from €60.
ENHUFR Whitening
🦷 What should I do if I have a toothache?
Toothache can indicate cavities, cracked teeth, or abscesses. We recommend our Emergency Care service for same-day treatment. Call +36 1 234 5678 immediately.
ENHUFR Emergency
💎 What are porcelain veneers?
Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front of teeth. They correct chips, discoloration, and shape. Prices from €350 per tooth.
ENHU Veneers
📅 How do I book an appointment?
You can book via: (1) This chatbot, (2) Call +36 1 234 5678, (3) Online form at dentalux.com/booking. Same-day slots available for emergencies.
ENHUFR Booking
🔬 How much do dental implants cost?
Dental implant prices start from €800 per implant. This includes the titanium post, abutment, and crown. Payment plans available. Free consultation included.
ENFR Implants
